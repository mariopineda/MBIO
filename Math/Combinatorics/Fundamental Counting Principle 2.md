How many ways are there of getting from A to C, passing through each point at most once?
![[Pathways.png]]


> [!success]+ Solution
> Route $A \rightarrow B_1 \rightarrow C$
> FCP: $1 \times 3 = 3$
> Route $A \rightarrow B_2 \rightarrow C$
> FCP: $2 \times 1 = 2$
> Route $A \rightarrow B_3 \rightarrow C$
> FCP: $3 \times 2 = 6$
>
> $3 + 2 + 6 = 11$ routes
